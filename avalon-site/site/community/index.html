Who We Are

These pages list many of the contributors to avalon. They have worked and are working long and hard to make quality software for the rest of the world to use. Contributors to the Avalon Project should be extremely proud of themselves! If you would like to become a contributor, please see the Get Involved document to figure out how. If you are a contributor but are not listed, submit a patch!

Process

Avalon, like all other projects at Apache, is a meritocracy and follows the Apache Way of community and project management. This page contains a bunch of references to the structure, operation and process aspects that help us maintain project oversight.
