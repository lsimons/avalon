J Aaron Farr

(farra at apache dot org)

Aaron started working with Avalon in 2002, quickly became a COP convert and joined the team as a committer in 2003. His initial work focused on testing Fortress and Merlin and providing documentation.

In addition to finishing a Scientific Computing degree at the University of Pittsburgh, Aaron works at Sony Electronics as a software developer. He is also active in other open source projects outside of Apache.
